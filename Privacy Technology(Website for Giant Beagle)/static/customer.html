Program Overview



Healthier Choices Program is an initiative by Giant Beagle to introduce its customers to the paradise of healthier choices that await them: discounts on low-sugar and low-calorie, prepared brand food product. This involves collecting customer data from our in-store pharmacy to identify health conditions of the customers and accordingly advertise suitable and healthy food choices to them. Our advertising partners will help us get the word out to our customers.
