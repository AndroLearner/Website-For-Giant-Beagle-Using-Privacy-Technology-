INFORMED CONSENT FORM

Description of Program

Healthier Choices Program is an initiative by Giant Beagle to introduce its customers to the paradise of healthier choices that await them: discounts on low-sugar and low-calorie, prepared brand food product. This involves collecting customer data from in-store pharmacy to identify health conditions of the customers and accordingly advertise suitable and healthy food choices to them. Our advertising partners will help us get the word out to our customers.

Participation

Participation is voluntary. You do not have to take part in this program if you do not want to do so. If you enroll for the program and then decide that you want to stop, you can do whatever you wish and there will not be any penalty.

Purpose of Data Collection

Our purpose for collecting this information is to provide you with a plethora of healthier food choices by sending out advertisements custom made for you. In order to accomplish this goal of better advertising, we will need to disclose your identifiable information. You have various data sharing options to choose from, such as basic information will enable us share just your name and address, general information will allow us to reveal your name, address, gender & age and medical information will help us in targeting your specific health codition by revealing all the above mentioned information along with your ailments, prescriptions, etc. You can either consent to reveal this identiable information or choose de-dentified information sharing where explicit consent is not required by the law.

Potential Benefits & Risk

You will receive direct benefit of healthier food choices and lucrative discounts on food products specific to your needs as per health conditions. There are no forseeable risks involved in participating in this program as we strictly comply to HIPPA Regulations for our entire workflow.

Record of Consent

Your signature on the Enrollment Form indicates that you have understood the information about the Healthier Choices Program and consent to your participation. You should have received a copy of the consent form for your own record. If you have further questions related to this program, please reach out to Giant Beagle's customer support.
